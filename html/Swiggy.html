About Swiggy


Swiggy is a new-age consumer-first organization offering an easy-to-use convenience
platform, accessible through a unified app.

Mission

Our mission is to elevate the quality of life of the urban
consumer by offering unparalleled convenience.
Convenience is what makes us tick. It’s what makes us
get out of bed and say, “Let’s do this.”

Industry Pioneer

Being among the first few entrants, Swiggy has
successfully pioneered the hyperlocal commerce
industry in India, launching Food Delivery in 2014 and
Quick Commerce in 2020. Due to the pioneering status
of Swiggy, it is well-recognised as a leader in innovation
in hyperlocal commerce and as a brand synonymous
with the categories it is present in.
